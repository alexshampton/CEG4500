## Implementation Methood
The stone face statue has interpolated shading using Gouraud shading and interpolated normals. The two lights used were point light and directional light.

## Initial Load 
    * Orthographic Projection: off
    * Perspective Projection: on
    * Light 1 (Point Light): on
    * Light 2 (Directional Light): on
    * Specular Relection: off
